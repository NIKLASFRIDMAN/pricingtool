How pricing works
  • Primary brand is charged at 100% of the selected module price.
  • Strategic brands are charged at 70%; additional secondary brands at 50%.
  • Included case volume is weighted at 100% for primary, 50% for strategic, and 20% for secondary brands.
  • Difficulty only changes included cases: Easy = high end, Medium = midpoint, Hard = low end.
  • Allocation frequency multiplies the subscription price: Daily 2.0x, Weekly 1.5x, Monthly 1.0x, Quarterly 0.8x.
  • Discount is based on selected modules × brands in scope: 2 = 5%, 3 = 10%, 4+ = 15%.
  • The Dedicated Incident Mailbox is a fixed 500 EUR client-level add-on when the Anti-Phishing Bundle is selected.
  • Extra cases are billed separately at 15 EUR per case.
Anti-Phishing Bundle

Module Description

Our Infringement & Fraud Response Team helps protect your brands against phishing, fraud, and trademark/copyright misuse. We provide a dedicated anti-phishing mailbox so your teams and partners can forward suspicious emails, SMS, and fraudulent websites for rapid review.

Service Goal

Our goal is to protect clients from phishing attacks by detecting, analyzing, and responding to threats quickly and effectively, thereby safeguarding sensitive information and mitigating financial and reputational damage.

Service Scope

Phishing Detection and Analysis: The team will use specialized tools and techniques to detect and analyze phishing attacks. We will monitor various sources of data, such as email, SMS, social media, and websites, to identify phishing attempts.

Incident Response: The team will have a plan in place to respond to phishing incidents quickly and effectively. This may involve taking steps to block the phishing site, contacting law enforcement, and working with other organizations to mitigate the impact of the attack.

Please note that the majority of incidents should be reported to us by client, given that Clients are often the first to notice unusual or suspicious activities related to their users and services, enabling quicker incident reporting and response.
