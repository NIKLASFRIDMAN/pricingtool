Enforcement and support

All packages include: friendly request letters, C&D letters, hosting & registrar complaints, platform IPR complaints, payment service provider complaints, search engine blacklisting, browser blocking, ICANN and ccTLD registry non-compliance escalation. Analyst review on all cases. Quarterly brand risk review. Fast infringement response (critical cases within 24 hours).

UDRP/URS proceedings available separately at 1,800 EUR per case (excluding arbitration center fees). Extra cases beyond the monthly allocation are billed at 15 EUR per case. Minimum order period: 3 months.
